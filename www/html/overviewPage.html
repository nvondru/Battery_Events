Battery Events

What is this app about?

Everytime you charge your phone this app will track information about the battery level in the background. You can inspect the tracked data in this app.

What information is tracked?

- Battery Level (when plugged/unplugged)

- GPS-Location (when plugged)

- Time (when plugged/unplugged)

You can always delete an element by clicking the trashcan symbol on the detail page. If you want to delete all elements, click the trashcan symbol on the overview page.
